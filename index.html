Taupo Drone Information

This map application has been designed to provide Drone pilots with aerodrome and council property information at any given location within the Taupo District.

The information this app provides can be used to assist in the filling out of the Taupo Airport Authority RPAS Agreement Form, and the Taupo District Council Drone Permit.

Within the app simply click, tap, locate (mobile only) or search in order to be provided with the relevant information for your location of interest.
